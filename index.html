How Does Gig Work !?!?!?

Gig aims to connect you with working professionals to help build meaningful collaborations based on mutual interests. We believe in work connections over transactional-based work. Gig’s unique and interactive filtering system helps match you with the perfect job! Our database will match Gig postings to your current set of filters and allow you to scroll through them. Simply click no on the ones you are not interested in, and yes to those you are! If the client says yes to your profile, then it’s a match! Check out our Phone App to match with hundreds of Gigs in your local area!
