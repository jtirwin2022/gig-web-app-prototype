TroubleShooting

Gig aims to help protect both freelancers and clients. It is unfortunate and understandably concerning when payment is either late or worse, missing. We suggest going through the following list:
1.Contact the client that hired you directly on our app or website built-in chat function.
2.Try sending the client an email.
3.Please allow 5 business days for the client to respond before submitting a dispute. To file a dispute, please follow the “How Do I Submit a Dispute” page and make sure to add the Gig I.D number to the message.

Fill out the Submit a Dispute form. Make sure to include your Gig I.D number along with a detailed description of what the dispute is regarding. Please note that if you do not allow for a 5 business day reply period from the client, your request will be cancelled. Once your request is submitted, the Gig team will respond to you within 3 business days. Disputes can take up a maximum of 15 days to resolve.
